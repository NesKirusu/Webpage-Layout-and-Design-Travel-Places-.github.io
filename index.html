D O M E S T I C

B O H O L

Bohol is known not just for its main island, but also the numerous smaller surrounding islands that are famous for their coral reefs. Bohol is also well known for its lush Chocolate Hills – a must-visit on your trip there!

C E B U

Known as the Philippines' Queen City of the South, Cebu is a treasure trove of activities for the whole family! Home to historical churches that have withstood the test of time, pay a visit to the famed Basilica de Sto. Nino and the Magellan's Cross iconic to this religious city.

P A L A W A N

Palawan Island is one of Philippines' hidden beauties located in the region of MIMAROPA. One of the best ways to explore the area is to go on island hopping tours and enjoy the tropical landscapes of Luli Island, Cowrie Island and Starfish Island - all perfect for snorkeling or a lazy day on the beach.

T A G A Y T A Y

With the hilly terrain, there are multiple spots to view the famous Taal Volcano and Lake. People’s Park in the Sky, found at the city’s highest point, is one of the most popular.
  I N T E R N A T I O N A L

H O N G K O N G

The harbor city of Hong Kong is renowned for its spectacular skylines and scenic views. A typical day in this vibrant city consists of the most amazing dim sum, retail therapy in Mong Kok and a slow stroll along the neon-lit streets when dusk hits.

T O K Y O

Tokyo is Japan’s bustling capital and the world’s most populous metropolis. Over 10 million foreign tourists visit this vibrant city every year and it only keeps increasing! A typical day of sightseeing in Tokyo can take you from historic temples to impressive neon-lit skyscrapers.
